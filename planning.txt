About
    Brief History
        Chrono Trigger was developed by Squaresoft, and the lead for the project called the Dream Team: the creator of the game Final Fantasy, Hironobu Sakaguchi; the creator of Dragon Quest, Yuji Horii; and the creator of the famous manga Dragon Ball, Akira Toriyama.

    Soundtrack
        The soundtrack for Chrono Trigger was composed mainly by Yasunori Mitsuda. Mitsuda was disappointed by his salary and his current position as a sound designer, and demanded that Squaresoft either make him a composer for a project, or he would quit. He would overwork himself to the point where he developed stomach ulcers and had to hand off the rest of the music to Final Fantasy's composer, Nobou Uematsu.
    Characters
        Chrono Trigger has a cast made up of people from different time periods. Crono is the main character of Chrono Trigger, and is the first character the player can control. He can wield Light magic. Crono is from the present, 1000 AD. In this time period, Crono meets two other people, Marle and Lucca. They can wield Ice and Fire magic respectively.
